ABOUT US

Valley View High School is a vibrant, inclusive high school known for its strong academic programs, diverse extracurricular offerings, and community-centered culture. Nestled in a scenic valley, the school boasts modern facilities, including state-of-the-art science labs, a performing arts center, and extensive athletic fields. With a dedicated team of teachers and staff, our school emphasizes both academic excellence and personal growth, encouraging students to pursue their passions and develop leadership skills. The school is also celebrated for its involvement in local community projects, making it a cornerstone of the surrounding area. We are located in Texas, United States of America.
